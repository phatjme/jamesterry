What I write about

We’re all constantly learning as we embark on untrodden territory in our startup journeys.

For a long time I’ve kept these learnings to myself, or never really fully contextualised them, into a valuable explanation.

That's why I started to write the occasional blog post.

The idea of this blog is to help founders navigate the myriad of issues they're bound to face. With everything from the foundations of starting a company, hiring the right people, finding product-market fit, scaling processes and techniques to relentlessly focus effort where it matters most.

If you find these posts helpful, do subscribe to my mailing list to stay in the loop!
