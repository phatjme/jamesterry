What I write about

We’re all constantly learning new things as we embark on untrodden or new territory in our startup journey.

For too long I’ve kept these learnings to myself, or never really fully contextualised them into somewhat of an explanation.

That's why I started to write the odd blog post.

This blog is mainly to help founders navigate the beginning of starting a company, scaling their product organisation and focusing their effort where it matters most.

If you find my posts helpful, do subscribe to my mailing list to stay up to date as I write.
