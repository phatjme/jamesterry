How I invest

I invest small tickets into early stage tech companies. I try to add value on top of my investment by providing support/advice where I can. I also tend to introduce my investments to my network of HNW angels, VCs and founders.

What I typically look for in new investments:

  • Incredible founder(s) - resilient, smart yet humble founders.
  • Radical simplicity - solving a single problem, really f*cking well.
  • Clear distribution strategy - an obvious distribution strategy or an inherently viral product.
  • Network effects - baked in to the way the product has been designed to build a moat.
