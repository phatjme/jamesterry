Advisor

What do I help with?

As a two-time startup CEO & board member coming from a background of product, design and development, I tend to be most helpful to tech startups that need support in the following areas:

  • Product strategy
  • Design & branding
  • Business modelling
  • Growth and marketing
  • Go-to-market strategy
  • Early stage foundations (raising money, hiring and strategy)

How does it work?

I never charge for advising. I strongly believe in giving back what you learn to the entrepreneurial ecosystem.

As an advisor you could count my support as and when you need it, whether over a call, a lunch, or a game of squash!

Obviously, time is limited. So I only work with companies that I’m excited about and feel I can add value. What I typically look for:

  • Product-first companies - you care, above all, about building a product that people value.
  • Early stage - you’re in the first few years of your journey, perhaps with a pre-seed/seed raise.
  • Traction & progress - you’re actually executing your idea and showing great progress.
  • Great people - you’re a team of super intelligent, resilient, humble people.

Interested in working together?

If you think we're a good fit, email me with a short blurb on what you do, who you are and where you’d like my help.

I may also be able to help with investment, if you're raising a round, see if you're a good fit here.
